About Us


Foodya is an Indian restaurant aggregator and food delivery start-up founded by Ritesh Vishwakarma and Shubham Singh Lodhi in 2020. Foodya provides information, menus and user-reviews of restaurants as well as food delivery options from partner restaurants in select cities. .

Order food online from restaurants and get it delivered.Today, Foodya is the leading food ordering and delivery platform in India. The innovative technology, large and nimble delivery service, and exceptional consumer focus at Foodya enabled a host of benefits that includes lightning fast deliveries,all while having the pleasure of enjoying your favourite meal wherever you'd like it.
